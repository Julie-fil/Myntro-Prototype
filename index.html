Your savings, protected by the EU

Eligible deposits are covered by the EU Deposit Guarantee Scheme up to €100,000 per customer, per institution. This framework applies across participating member states and is designed to give retail savers a standardized safety net.

Myntro partners with regulated credit institutions. Our operations are subject to oversight consistent with European financial standards. Where applicable, partner activities fall under supervision by authorities such as the Swedish Financial Supervisory Authority (Finansinspektionen)—supporting transparency and compliance for investors.
