Typography (see index.html)
=============================
- Headlines: Playfair Display (700) — section titles, hero H1, logo, step numerals, calculator headline.
- Subheadlines: Source Sans Pro semibold — eyebrow labels, intro lines under H2, calculator mode copy.
- Body & UI: Source Sans Pro — nav, buttons, inputs, paragraphs, cards, footer.

Fonts load from Google Fonts; no files required in this folder.
